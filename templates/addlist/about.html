About Assignment Tracker

Track, manage, and stay ahead of all your school assignments — effortlessly!

  • 📚 View a list of all assignments
  • 📝 Add new assignments
  • ⏰ View assignments that are due soon
  • ✅ Track progress of assignments

It has been designed with a focus on simplicity, usability, and flexibility, so you can stay on top of all your deadlines.
